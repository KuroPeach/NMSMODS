Added snap points for various common equipment and buildings, allowing them to snap to each other, themselves, large floors, small floors, large prefabs, small modular rooms, cargo ship holds, and external platforms.

Now integrated with the Snappy module, with permission from EMPTY.d. See the notes below for details. This work was done by KuroPeach.

For players who know how to use AMUMSS, it is now easier to add the buildings you want through LUA, just find the corresponding file path and add it to the corresponding size list.

Remember: scaling will break snapping! !

Added 5 snap points for large floors, 1 for small floors, 5 for small modular rooms, and 9 for various cargo ship cabins and external platforms.
